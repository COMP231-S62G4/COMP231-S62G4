WeMeet Help >> Contacts

Use Contacts link from the swipe menu to go to Contacts screen.

This screen has similar function as phone's contacts list.

This screen contains
1. A textbox to enter filter query.
2. A serach button to filter contacts based on your query

You can long press any contact to explore more options, such as:

1. Share location (To share your location with the contact).

2. Unshare location (To stop location sharing with the contact).

2. Send Invite (To send invitation SMS to contact, SMS charges applied).
