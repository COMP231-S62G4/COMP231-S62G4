WeMeet Help >> Friends Nearby

Friends Nearby is the first screen that you will see once you register your phone number.

This screen displays Google Map and loads contacts that are nearby on the map, provided they have shared their location with you.

1. If your contact have profile image set up, it will be loaded on map otherwise it will show as default blue icon.

2. Map has this button to zoom into your current location.

3. You can use zoom controls to zoom in and out of map.
